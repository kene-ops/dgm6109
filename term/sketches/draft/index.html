Dataset Description

My dataset consists of daily personal observations collected over a two-week period. Each daily record includes measurements of sleep duration, meditation duration, number of meals, gym/workout level, and productivity level. The goal of collecting this data is to ascertain how my gym/workout level affect my level of productivity. Hypothesis: as my gym level decreases, my level of productivity decreases. Additionally, sleeping duration, feeding and meditation might also have impact on my productivity.

Each observation is recorded as a single day, allowing analysis of correlations between gym, sleep, meditation, feeding and productivity level.

Data Variables, Units, and Ranges

  • Sleep: Minutes per night (240 – 480 minutes)
  • Meditation: Minutes per day (10 – 18 minutes)
  • Meals: Count per day (1 – 3 meals)
  • Gym / Workout: Minutes per day (0 – 240 reps)
  • Productivity: Set Tasks /tasks completed (1 – 5 scale)

Sketch 1 – Multi-Dimensional Scatterplot / Bubble Plot

Hypothesis

As my daily gym/workout level decreases, my productivity level also decreases. Additionally, reduced sleep duration and fewer meals further lower productivity.

Data Elements Used

  • Gym (number of reps)
  • Productivity (1 – 5)
  • Sleep duration (minutes)
  • Meals (count/day)

Visual Representation

  • X-axis: Gym (reps)
  • Y-axis: Sleep duration (minutes)
  • Colored Rectangles: Productvity
  • Dots Number of meals

This visualization builds on the scatterplot and bubble plot concepts practiced in class while creatively extending them to incorporate multiple visual encodings with rectangular shape.

Sketch 2 – Productivity Heatmap

Hypothesis

As my meal consumption improves, my productivity level improves. Additionally, increased meditation duration might further increase productvity level.

Data Elements Used

  • Meditation duration (minutes)
  • Productivity (1 – 5)
  • Meals (count/day)

Visual Representation

  • X-axis: Meals consumed
  • Y-axis: Meditation duration (minutes)
  • Cell color: Productivity level

This heatmap allows rapid detection of patterns and correlations between meditation and feeding while revealing their combined influence on productivity.

Sketch 3 – Creative Visualization

Research Question

Which factor or variable affects my productivity level: sleep, gym, or feeding?

Data Elements Used

  • Gym level
  • Productivity level
  • Sleep duration
  • Meals

Visual Representation

  • Vertical line(s): Gym level
  • Flower pod: Productivity level
  • Color: Sleep duration
  • Dots: Number of meals

Each day is represented as a cluster, creating a visually and holistic representation of daily observations. This creative approach is inspired by experimental data visualization from the “Dear Data”.
