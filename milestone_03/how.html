How Prime Ride Works

Simple, safe, and reliable transportation for essential workers.

1. Sign Up

Create an account and verify your identity.

2. Book a Ride

Enter pickup, destination, and preferred time.

3. Get Matched

We connect you with a verified nearby driver.

4. Track & Ride

Track your driver in real-time and ride safely.

5. Arrive Safely

Reach your destination on time with peace of mind.

6. Rate Experience

Provide feedback to maintain quality service.
