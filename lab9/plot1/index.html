As my gym level increases, my productivity level increases.

Hypothesis: As my gym level increases, my level of productivity increases. Additionally, longer duration of sleep and meditation might have a positive effect on my level of productivity.

This scatterplot tests whether high gym level, combined with higher sleeping time can lead to higher productivity. For a better focus, i used Array.filter() to filter off the days i didn't workout to focus on the effect of workout days + sleep. I visualized Productivity on y axis, gym on x axis, sleep duration with circles and meditation with colors.
