Technical Explanation

How I used Array.filter()

In Plot 1, I used Array.filter() to create a more focused observations. I specifically filtered the data to remove days where i didn't go to the gym. This was useful because i wanted to see if the effect of the combination of gym and High-Sleep on my level of productivity.

How I used Array.sort()

In Plot 2, I used Array.sort() to organize my data based on the workout intensity (reps). In D3.js, elements are drawn in order; by sorting the largest values to appear first in the array, I ensured that smaller circles are drawn on top of larger ones. This prevents data loss where a small bubble might be hidden behind a large one.
